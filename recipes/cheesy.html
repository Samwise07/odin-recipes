Cheesy Venison Potatos

Description

A feel good meal for those of you who harvest your own meat. Could be used as an appetizer for the big game or a family dinner all by itself.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground venison
  • 1 cup shredder cheddar cheese
  • 4 potatos
  • 1/3 cups diced onions
  • 1/3 cup sliced jalapeño
  • 1/3 cup sliced green onions
  • salt and pepper (to taste)
  • sour cream

Step by Step Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 400 degrees
  2. Bake potatos in oven for 45 minutes
  3. Cook ground venison in skillet on medium heat, season with salt and pepper to taste.
  4. While potatos and venison are cooking slice and dice your vegetables.
  5. After potatos are cooked, cut them in half and lay them face up. Layer cooked ground venison on top of potatos and then cheese. Put them back into the oven for five minutes.
  6. Remove potatos from oven and add onions, green onions, and jalapeño
  7. Let cool for 2 minutes and serve with a side of sour cream.
